Stream team is a brand new company dedicated to bringing the best video sharing expierence to its users. Discover, Share, and be informed about what others are sharing.

From the Homepage click on the "Sign-in" button. Select the new user link. Follow the step by step instructions for makeing an account. Enjoy the videos!

Stream Team was founded by 3 classmates all taking the same Web design class. What started out to be a group project soon grew into a popular social platform for sharing videos. Stream team continues to grow today.

Stream team is a place to put your personally recorded videos. Videos that are copywrited should not be posted. Videos should be appropriate for children as our website is open for all ages.

Every video that is posted on Stream Team is filtered to ensure that it follows coorporate policy. Videos with Copyright enfringment or that contain graphic or sexual content will be removed.

If you are struggling to login to your account then navigate to the Sign-in page (if not there already) and then click the forgot your password link. This will prompt you to enter your username. An email will then be sent to your email attached with your Stream Team account with a link to reset your password.

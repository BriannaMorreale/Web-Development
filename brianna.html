Working with Stream Team

Brianna joined the Stream Team with the vision of making a website that would be liked by many and would showcase interesting entertainment. When discussing the overall design, they had all agreed that a slick, dark design would be appealing to viewers' eyes while also matching the aesthetic of the audience watching the videos. In order to create the website, the team had to come together and use HTML, CSS, and Javascript to display what you can currently find on the site. Brianna and the Stream Team felt that the concept of the website would be very popular amongst a younger audience and therefore be easily popularized. In addition, they wanted the ability for their audience to get more out of their website, and thus, the ability to buy a subscription was created. By developing a subscription, the viewers would be able to benefit even more by getting content and even funnier videos that a free account would not be able to see. Brianna overall found the idea of creating the website as a whole to be an intricate challenge that would produce a professional outcome that would be needed by many.
